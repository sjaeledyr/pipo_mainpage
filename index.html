PROJECT: Party Cannon

Project Party Cannon was created to allow different skill sets to flourish and thrive on the server, giving people whose talents may not lie in building another possible role within the community. It also deepens the rank of "Builder" by allowing for more growth within the role. --All credit of PROJECT: Party Cannon belongs to SunsetShimmer--
